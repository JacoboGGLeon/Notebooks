    \paragraph{Boxplots}\label{boxplots}

Boxplots are another way to understand the distribution of a numerical
variable. It specifies something called quartiles.

If the numbers in a distribution with 100 numbers are arranged in an
increasing order; * the 1st quartile will occupy the 25th position, *
the 3rd quartile will occupy the 75th position, and so on. * The median
will be the average of the 50th and 51st terms. * Median is the middle
term when the numbers in the distribution are arranged in the increasing
order. * Mode is the one that occurs with the maximum frequency, * while
mean is the sum of all the numbers divided by their total count.
